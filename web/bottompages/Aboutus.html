A Leading Issuer of Credit Cards in India
A leading player in the Indian credit card market, SAHARA Cards is a joint venture between Sahara, the country's oldest and largest bank, and GE Capital to offer Indian consumers extensive access to a wide range of world-class, value-added payment products and services.
The partnership is operated through 2 joint-venture companies - SAHARA Cards & Payment Services Pvt. Ltd., which focuses on the marketing and distribution of SAHARA Cards and GE Capital Business Processes Management Services Pvt. Ltd., which handles the technology and processing needs of SAHARA Cards.
With a current customer base of over 2.1 million, SAHARA Cards operates through a footprint of 22 cities in India (not including the reach of our remittances, delivery and collections infrastructure).
